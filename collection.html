TEAM REDBULL

Red Bull Racing is owned by the energy drink company Red Bull. Based in the UK, the team, led by Christian Horner, is known for its aggressive racing style and innovative strategies. With star driver Max Verstappen, they have won multiple Championships and are recognized for their dynamic and competitive approach in the world of Formula 1.

TEAM MERCEDES

Mercedes-AMG Petronas is a dominant force in Formula 1, known for technological innovation and excellence. Led by team principal Toto Wolff, the team, based in Brackley, UK, has consistently achieved success with star driver Lewis Hamilton. Renowned for cutting-edge engineering, Mercedes has set new standards in the sport, securing multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships.

TEAM FERRARI

Scuderia Ferrari, established in 1929 and based in Maranello, Italy, is an iconicly known for its rich history and passionate racing tradition. Led by team principal Mattia Binotto, Ferrari has a legacy of Championships and is recognized for its iconic red cars and prancing horse logo. With a commitment to performance, Ferrari remains a perennial contender and symbol of excellence in Formula 1.

Learn more

TEAM McLAREN

McLaren, founded in 1963 and based in Woking, UK, is known for innovation and performance. Led by team principal Andreas Seidl, McLaren has a rich history with iconic drivers and multiple Championships. Recognized for its distinctive orange livery, McLaren's commitment to technological excellence and partnership with Mercedes has fueled its recent competitiveness in Formula 1.

TEAM ASTON MARTIN

Aston Martin Cognizant, was rebranded from Racing Point in 2021, is a British-based Formula 1 team headquartered in Silverstone. With Lawrence Stroll as a key figure, Aston Martin returned to F1 with a focus on performance and style. The team's distinct green livery and affiliation with the luxury car brand Aston Martin bring a unique flair to the grid. While not as historically entrenched in F1 as some counterparts, the team aims to make its mark with a competitive edge and a touch of British elegance.

Learn more

TEAM ALPINE

Alpine, part of the Renault Group, is a French-based Formula 1 team headquartered in the UK. Formerly known as Renault, the team rebranded as Alpine in 2021. Led by Executive Director Marcin Budkowski, Alpine's distinct blue and white livery reflects its French heritage. With drivers like Esteban Ocon and Fernando Alonso, the team aims to blend automotive expertise with a commitment to performance in Formula 1.

TEAM WILLIAMS

Williams Racing, founded in 1977 and based in Grove, UK, is a historic Formula 1 team with a rich legacy. Despite recent challenges, including changes in leadership, Williams remains a respected name in F1, known for past successes with legendary drivers like Ayrton Senna. The team emphasizes a commitment to performance and innovation on the Formula 1 grid.

TEAM ALPHA TAURI

Scuderia AlphaTauri, based in Faenza, Italy, is affiliated with Red Bull Racing. Formerly Toro Rosso, it was rebranded as AlphaTauri in 2020. Led by Franz Tost, the team is known for nurturing young talent and sports a distinctive white and navy blue livery. AlphaTauri combines Italian flair with Red Bull innovation on the Formula 1 grid.

TEAM ALPHA ROMEO

Alfa Romeo Racing Orlen, based in Hinwil, Switzerland, is associated with the iconic Italian brand. Led by Frédéric Vasseur, the team's red and white livery reflects Alfa Romeo's heritage. With a mix of experienced and talented drivers, they aim for competitive performances in Formula 1, blending Italian automotive style with a focus on success and innovation.

Learn more

TEAM HAAS

Haas F1 Team, founded by Gene Haas in 2016, is based in the USA with a presence in both North Carolina and the UK. Recognized for its distinctive black and gold livery, Haas collaborates with Ferrari for power units. Led by Guenther Steiner, the team emphasizes talent development and competitiveness in Formula 1.
